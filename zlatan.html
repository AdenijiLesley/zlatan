Background History of Zlatan

Omoniyi Temidayo Raphael aka zlatan Ibile hails from Ekiti state. He was born 19 December 1994. zlatan happens to be from a fervent christain home, his father being a pastor at Redeem Christain Church of God, which implies he was brought up following the doctrine of christainty that his famil strongly holds.

In essense it shows he would have been a church boy while growing up, owning to that expectations he would haave been a very active member. Thus he joined the church band as a drummer. Be as it may, he wasnt born with a silver spoon and had to do menial jobs like bricklaying, grasscutting and the other related works to support himself and his family, which signifies he has been an hustler from his young age.

His Career

His carrer was said to have kicked up at 19 years of his age. His journey to fame was quite a swift one, and it started when he followed one of his friends to the studio to record and remeber he was in the chior so he knew a bit about music and had gained experince along the way, this helped boost his passion, and this was shortly after his secondary school completion.

By the time he gained admission into the university, he already pictured himself as a musician. His first experience with the mic played out when he got into a campus one mic tour, a music competition organised in his school by airtel, and as we would have it, he put in a very good show and wowed the audience with his talent, thus emerging the winner and driving home a luxurious car. This act watered the ground for his later exploits in the music industry

As soon as this great talent stormed the music industry, he was easly identified by his catch phrase 'kapaichumarimaichopaco'. In 2016, he recoreded thensong 'odun yi'. In 2017, also he dropped another hit track titled 'omoge', fortunately, this hit grew more than his first release. Yet these two songs were not able to give him his desired recognition nationally in the industry.

Luckily for Zlatan,he got the oportunity to feature the afrobeat and hiphop superstar Olamide and they released the song 'my body' in the same year of 2017. This song became a gam-changer as it changed things for Zlatan and made him known nationally. As a result of this song, the attention of Mr Lawrence Irabo who is a co-owner of A.B.E and decided to sign him into the record label in year 2018.
